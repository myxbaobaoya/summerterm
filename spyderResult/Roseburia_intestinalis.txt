
Roseburia intestinalis is a saccharolytic, butyrateproducing bacterium first isolated from human faeces. It is anaerobic, grampositive, nonsporeforming, slightly curved rodshaped and motile by means of multiple subterminal flagella. LT  DSM T  NCIMB T is the type strain.
