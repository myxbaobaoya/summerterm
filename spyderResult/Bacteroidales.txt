
Bacteroidales is an order of bacteria. Notably it includes the genera Prevotella and Bacteroides , which are commonly found in the human gut microbiota.
The currently accepted taxonomy is based on the List of Prokaryotic names with Standing in Nomenclature and the phylogeny is based on wholegenome sequences.a
