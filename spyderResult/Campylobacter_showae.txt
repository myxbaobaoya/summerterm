
Campylobacter showae is a species of Campylobacter found in humans. It is gramnegative, straight rodshaped, motile by means of multiple unipolar flagella. It is asaccharolytic and prefers an anaerobic atmosphere. SU A  ATCC  is its type strain. Its genome has been sequenced.
