The Burkholderiales order Pseudomonadota. Like Pseudomonadota, Gramnegative. They include several pathogenic bacteria, including species Burkholderia, Bordetella, Ralstonia. They also include Oxalobacter related genera, unusual using oxalic acid source carbon. Other wellstudied genera include Alcaligenes, Cupriavidus, Achromobacter, Comamonas, Delftia, Massilia, Duganella, Janthinobacterium, Polynucleobacter important freshwater bacterioplankton, nonpathogenic Paraburkholderia, Caballeronia, Polaromonas, Thiomonas, Collimonas, Hydrogenophaga, Sphaerotilus, Variovorax, Acidovorax, Rubrivivax Rhodoferax members photosynthetic purple nonsulfur bacteria, Herbaspirillum capable nitrogenfixation.
