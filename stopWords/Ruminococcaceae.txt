Oscillospiraceae family bacteria class Clostridia. All Oscillospiraceae obligate anaerobes. However, members family diverse shapes, rodshaped others cocci. Within family, Faecalibacterium prausnitzii notable abundant commensal bacteria human gut microbiota. In addition, several members Ruminococcus found human gut.
