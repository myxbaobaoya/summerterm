Betacoronavirus  is a species of coronavirus which infects humans and cattle The infecting virus is an enveloped positivesense singlestranded RNA virus and is a member of the genus Betacoronavirus and subgenus Embecovirus Like other embecoviruses it has an additional shorter spikelike surface protein called hemagglutinin esterase HE as well as the larger coronavirus spike protein
