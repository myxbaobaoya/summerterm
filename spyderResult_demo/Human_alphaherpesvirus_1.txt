Herpes simplex virus
Human alphaherpesvirus  HHV is a species of virus in the genus Simplexvirus subfamily Alphaherpesvirinae family Herpesviridae and order Herpesvirales
The Human alphaherpesvirus  virus can be classified into six clades Four of these occur in East Africa one in East Asia and one in Europe and North America This suggests that the virus may have originated in East Africa The most recent common ancestor of the Eurasian strains appears to have evolved  years ago
